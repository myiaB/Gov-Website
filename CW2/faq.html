FAQ

Q: Who is eligible for the scheme?

A: All small busineses are eligible to apply, however only a select few will be successful.


Q: When is the closing date?

A: The closing date is the 16/12/2022.


Q: Which scheme should i choose?

A: The scheme you choose should directly benefit and be in demand within your company.


Q: Does the business owner have to apply or can it be any employee?

A: No.The business owner must apply for the fund or else the application will not be verified.


Q: Who decides if i get the fund?

A: The goverment has a specific committee created in order to select tghe candiates.They will look at all aspects of your business and ensure those who need it are selected for the funding.
